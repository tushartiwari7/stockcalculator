[Stock Calculator](https://stockcalculator.vercel.app)

Find out the current price of your investments in any stock worldwide. <br />
You just need to Choose the specific stock and enter the purchase date and quantity in which you bought it. <br/>
Then you will came to know the current value of the investments in that stock giving Profit/Loss statements with Absolute Returns too as shown below.
